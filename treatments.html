Aromatherapy

Treatment time: 60 minutes

Price: 20£


Aromatherapy is the use of essential oils to promote healing and relaxation. This holistic approach encompassed body, mind and spirit. The massage therapist uses up to five oils in a mixture, and chooses the oils based on what you need. Here are some widely used essential oils and their purported properties: Calming: chamomile, lavender, and geranium oil. Uplifting: ylang-ylang, clary sage, rose, and neroli oil. Energizing: rosemary oil. Decongesting: eucalyptus, pine, and tea tree oil.
